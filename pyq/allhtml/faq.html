Frequently Asked Questions

+

What is PyQ Library?

PyQ Library is an online platform where you can access previous year question papers from various colleges to help you prepare for exams.

+

How can I upload my own question papers?

To upload your own question papers, simply log in to your account and navigate to the "Upload" section. You can upload PDFs of your question papers easily.

+

Is the content free to access?

Yes, PyQ Library provides free access to all the uploaded question papers for students. You can download them without any charges.

+

Do I need to create an account to access the papers?

No, you do not need an account to download question papers. However, creating an account allows you to upload papers and keep track of your downloads.
